P.E.R.C.Y (Modular Attachment Car Kit)

January 2023 Automotive, Engineering, Flood Solutions

A modular attachment that converts regular cars into amphibious vehicles for flood situations.

Project Overview

P.E.R.C.Y (Portable Emergency Rescue Conversion for You) is an innovative modular kit that transforms standard automobiles into amphibious vehicles capable of navigating floodwaters. Designed with urban flooding scenarios in mind, this system provides an affordable alternative to specialized amphibious vehicles.

Key Features

  • Inflatable Buoyancy Chambers: Deploy within minutes to provide necessary flotation
  • Depth Sensing System: Automatically activates when water reaches dangerous levels
  • Waterproof Propulsion: Compact outboard motor attachment for water navigation
  • Universal Mounting: Adaptable to most sedan and SUV models
  • Emergency Signaling: Integrated LED strobe lights for rescue situations

Development Process

The project went through several iterations:

  1. Concept Phase: Research on flood patterns and existing solutions
  2. Prototyping: 3D-printed models and small-scale water tests
  3. Material Selection: Testing various polymers for durability and buoyancy
  4. Field Testing: Real-world trials with different vehicle types
  5. User Feedback: Incorporating suggestions from emergency responders

Potential Applications

  • Emergency evacuation during urban flooding
  • Disaster response vehicle augmentation
  • Rural transportation in flood-prone areas
  • Military and peacekeeping operations
